Comfortable & Clean Accommodations

Our hotel rooms are designed with your comfort in mind. Each room is clean, spacious, and equipped with cozy bedding to help you rest after a long journey or a day of temple visits. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a family, we have room options to suit your needs — all maintained with high standards of hygiene and care.

Scenic Views & Peaceful Ambience

Wake up to stunning views of the Himalayan mountains or the nearby river from your room. The peaceful surroundings and fresh mountain air create a relaxing atmosphere that’s perfect for unwinding. With minimal noise and a spiritual vibe in the air, our rooms offer a restful retreat for both body and mind.!
