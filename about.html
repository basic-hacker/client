About US

Welcome to SHREE PALACE -, your tranquil retreat nestled in the sacred town of Badrinath. Established with a heartfelt mission to serve pilgrims, travelers, and nature lovers, our hotel aims to offer much more than just a place to rest — we strive to provide an experience that nourishes your spirit, comforts your body, and connects you with the serene beauty of the Himalayas.
Our Mission - Our primary goal is to create a warm and welcoming environment where every guest feels respected, cared for, and rejuvenated. We understand that visiting Badrinath is a spiritual journey, and we are dedicated to supporting that journey with excellent service, comfortable accommodations, and a peaceful atmosphere.
Commitment to Quality - We maintain the highest standards of cleanliness, hygiene, and safety to ensure your stay is worry-free. Our rooms are thoughtfully designed to provide cozy comfort, while our attentive staff is always available to assist you with your needs — from travel guidance to personalized hospitality.
Sustainable Practices - Respecting the pristine environment around us is a core value at SHREE PALACE. We actively practice eco-friendly measures such as minimizing waste, conserving water, and using sustainable products. We believe that protecting the natural beauty of Badrinath is essential for future generations to experience the same spiritual and natural wonders.
Local Culture & Cuisine - Our hotel embraces the rich culture and traditions of Garhwal. We offer authentic local cuisine that allows guests to savor the unique flavors of the region. Beyond food, we encourage cultural exchange and provide insights into local customs, festivals, and history, enriching your overall experience.
A Place for All Travelers - Whether you are a pilgrim seeking divine blessings, a family on vacation, or an adventurer exploring the Himalayas, our hotel caters to your specific needs. With comfortable rooms, peaceful surroundings, and convenient access to key attractions, we make sure your stay is as smooth and enjoyable as possible.
Your Comfort, Our Priority - At the heart of everything we do is our commitment to your satisfaction. From the moment you arrive until your departure, our friendly team is dedicated to making your stay memorable. We believe hospitality is not just a service but a sincere expression of care and respect.
Come, experience the harmony of tradition and comfort at SHREE PALACE, where every stay is a step toward inner peace and relaxation. We look forward to welcoming you to our family and being part of your unforgettable journey in Badrinath.
